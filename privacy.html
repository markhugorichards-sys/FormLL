3. Why We Use Your Data

We use your personal data for the following purposes:

We do not use your data for unsolicited marketing. We will not sell, rent or share your personal data with third parties for their own marketing purposes.

4. Who We Share Data With

We share your data only as necessary to deliver the service:

  • Email service providers: For sending confirmation and case update emails

All third parties we share data with are required to handle it in accordance with UK GDPR.

5. How Long We Keep Your Data

We retain your personal data for a maximum of 3 years following the completion of your case, after which it is securely deleted. This retention period is necessary to handle any queries, complaints or legal matters that may arise.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at hello@formll.co.uk. We will respond within 30 days.
